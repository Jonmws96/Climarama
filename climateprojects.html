Jari Para, Brazil

Everyone with their own forest on Climarama is funding the Jari Para conservation project.

The project is protecting a patch of forest twice the size of Luxembourg that would have been deforested otherwise!

Alto Huayabamba Conservation

Protecting a major city sized area of high alpine forest where the Amazon Rainforest meets Alpine Tundra.

There are ten unique ecosystems with many endangered species benefitting from protection of and research in the forest!

Madre De Dios, Peru

Located in Southeast Peru this is a conservation project with small amounts of sustainable timber harvest.

The project protects a number of endangered species and rare trees as well as education programmes and outreach for local communities!

Brazil Nut Concessions

This is a conservation project that is directly stopping deforestation by creating a collaboration between 300 small concessioners and the developer.

It focuses on economic empowerment by creating a local economy based on protecting the Amazon Rainforest rather than illegally cutting and logging it!

Fazenda Sao Nicolao

Reforestation, revegetation and afforestation in an area cut down for coffee & cattle production in the 1980's and 90's

Replanting a forest half the size of manhattan and conserving an area almost three times the size!

Tist Uganda

Consisting of over 1,500 small farm plots reforesting and planting trees in one of the most aggressively deforested countries in the world

Forecast to have no forests left at all by 2050 - restoring forests here is of critical importance creating an environmentally and economically sustainable future
